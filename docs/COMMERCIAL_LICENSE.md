## Why a Commercial License?

MonokerOS is dual-licensed under:

1. **AGPL v3** — Free for open-source use, with copyleft requirements
2. **Commercial License** — For organizations that need to keep their modifications proprietary

### When You Need a Commercial License

You **must** obtain a commercial license if:

- You deploy MonokerOS as part of a proprietary SaaS product
- You modify MonokerOS and cannot disclose your source code
- Your organization has a policy prohibiting AGPL-licensed software
- You need to embed MonokerOS in closed-source products
- You require indemnification or warranty

You **do not** need a commercial license if:

- You use MonokerOS internally with no external distribution
- You comply with AGPL v3 by open-sourcing your modifications
- You are a hobbyist, student, or open-source project
